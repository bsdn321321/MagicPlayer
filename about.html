MagicPlayer

The MagicPlayer is a media player based on "libvlc 5.3.1", "libvlccore 5.1.0" and "Qt 4.8". High-intergration with PT Website is the main features of this media player. And because of the libvlc and QT,MagicPlayer can handle any video format and is platform-independent. If you are a high-quality-video ethusiast, you should have a try.
